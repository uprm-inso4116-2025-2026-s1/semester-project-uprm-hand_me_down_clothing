=== *1.5 Derived Goals*

Derived goals provide higher-level motivations that inform the design of epics and features.  
Each derived goal links to the epics it directly influences.

[[DG-1]]
*DG-1: Promote sustainability literacy and circular practices in Mayag√ºez*  
The project shall normalize reuse, repair, and responsible disposal behaviors among students and families through donation and resale norms.  
*Supports:* <<EPIC-1>>, <<EPIC-4>>  
*Broader impact:* item lifecycles will be extended and textile waste pressure will be reduced.

[[DG-2]]
*DG-2: Strengthen community engagement and mutual aid through UPRM-led outreach*  
The project will cultivate equitable sharing practices (donation, fair resale) centered on UPRM as the primary touchpoint.  
*Supports:* <<EPIC-2>>, <<EPIC-5>>, <<EPIC-6>>  
*Broader impact:* social capital will increase and households will respond more effectively to clothing and accessory needs.

[[DG-3]]
*DG-3: Raise awareness of affordability and access constraints faced by local households*  
The project shall make visible how structured sharing reduces acquisition cost and effort for students and families.  
*Supports:* <<EPIC-1>>, <<EPIC-3>>  
*Broader impact:* schools and neighborhood groups will make more informed choices about drives and targeted outreach.

These derived goals guide outreach, education, and validation activities alongside the primary objectives.
